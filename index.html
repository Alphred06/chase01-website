    • About the artist

    The Script are an Irish rock band formed in 2007 in Dublin, Ireland.
    They first released music in 2008. It consists of lead vocalist and
    keyboardist Daniel O'Donoghue, lead guitarist Mark Sheehan, and
    drummer Glen Power. The band moved to London after signing to
    Sony Label Group imprint Phonogenic and released their
    eponymous debut album in August 2008, preceded by the successful
    singles "The Man Who Can't Be Moved" and "Breakeven". Their next
    three albums, Science & Faith (2010), #3 (2012) and No Sound Without
    Silence (2014), all topped the album charts in Ireland and the UK,
    while Science & Faith reached number three in the US. Hit singles from
    the albums include "For the First Time", "Nothing", "Hall of Fame" and
    "Superheroes". The band's fifth studio album, Freedom Child, was
    released on 1 September 2017, and features the UK Top 20 single "Rain".
